Halo: A New Life

    Halo: A New Life is the current working title of a fanfiction project that I'm working on. Set in the Halo universe in the year 2553, it follows retired SPARTAN-III supersoldier Anthony-B256 as he tries to adjust himself to a new civilian life.

    Currently there is no ETA on when this will be finished but I hope to publish the first few chapters on either this site or AO3 by the end of the year. So stay tuned I guess, or not, I really don't care.
